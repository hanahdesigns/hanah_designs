About Us

Hanah Designs the innovators in the market for handicraft offer customers’ every choice in terms of various materials such as wood, metal, resin, mother of pearl, stone etc. We have sophisticated one-stop-shop products to address the specific needs of our customers and help to build a platter of opportunities where you have more options to choose from. We have progressed to a point where we cater from exclusive designers to mainstream wholesalers, retailers as well as importers.

Our team of experts work on a regular basis to ensure that our products are tailored to the latest trends and comply with credible national and international standards. We are committed to achieving the highest standards when meeting the needs of our customers through competitive pricing, quality, and reliable delivery. By leveraging our global network and market knowledge, we have the capacity and flexibility to rapidly respond to evolving trends in consumer and production markets.
